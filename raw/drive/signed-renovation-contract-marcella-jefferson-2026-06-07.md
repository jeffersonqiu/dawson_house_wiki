RENOVATION AGREEMENT

THIS AGREEMENT is made on the 31st day of March 2026 between:

(1) Marcella Callista Risye and Jefferson Qiu of 86 Dawson Rd, #05-03 SkyVille @ Dawson,

AND

(2) BUD STUDIO PTE. LTD. (Company Registration No. 202114713D) of 195 Pearl's Hill Terrace
#02-06 Singapore 168976 (hereinafter referred to as the "Contractor") of the other party.

WHEREAS:
(A) The Owner requires renovating and/or decorating of the premises located at 86 Dawson Rd,
#05-03 SkyVille @ Dawson, Singapore, 141086 ("Premises") and is engaging the services of the
Contractor for this purpose.
(B) The Contractor accepts such appointment and is willing and able to carry out the work
described in the Scope of Works attached and in accordance with the plans, drawings and
specifications specified in Appendix A ("Works").

NOW IT IS HEREBY AGREED as follows:

SCOPE OF WORKS
1. The Contractor agrees and undertakes to carry out and complete the Works to the reasonable
satisfaction of the Owner and in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
2. Subject to the Owner obtaining the relevant authorities' approval (if any), the Contractor
shall carry out and complete the Works within 100 days from the commencement of the Works
("Completion Date").
3. The performance of the Works shall not be inclusive of: (a) making any submissions to the
relevant authorities in relation to the Works; (b) installation of any accessories, furniture or
fixtures sourced by the Owner which is not part of the Works — unless otherwise agreed in writing.
4. Any modifications to the Works shall not be accepted unless agreed to in writing ("Change Order"),
which shall include the work/services modified and the costs/deductions for such modification.
5. The Owner shall be fully responsible and liable for increased costs due to mold, insects,
building code violations, or other discoveries not visible/apparent in the initial assessment.
The Contractor shall issue a Change Order for the Owner's approval; no further work performed
until agreement on addressing unforeseen issues.

PAYMENT AND PROCEDURE
6. The Owner agrees to pay the Contractor the sum of S$72,805.00 (inclusive of all service
charges and prevailing government taxes and GST) (the "Contract Sum") per Appendix B.
7. The Contract Sum does NOT include: costs for endorsement by a professional engineer,
electrical works, air-con works, sanitary fittings & accessories (light fixtures, curtains, etc)
unless otherwise agreed.
8. (a) The Owner shall, upon execution, pay 10% of the Contract Sum (the "Down Payment").
The Contractor commences Works only upon receipt of the Down Payment.
   (b) Delay/default in payment → Contractor may cease/suspend work, not liable for delay.
   (c) Late payment beyond 2 weeks → interest accrues at 1% per month on overdue sum.
9. Variation of Works (Change Order): additional costs require full payment before work commences;
cost reductions reflected at next payment milestone or reimbursed within 2 weeks.
10. Cancellation by Owner after execution → Contractor may appropriate/apply Down Payment toward
loss/damage; does not preclude other rights/remedies.
11. All cheques crossed and payable to "Bud Studio Pte. Ltd." Cash payment must be verified
with management; Owner liable for loss arising from such payment.

ACCESS TO OWNER'S PREMISES / PERMITS AND APPROVALS
12. Owner permits Contractor & agents free access to Premises at reasonable hours.
13. Contractor assists Owner in obtaining necessary renovation permits; expenses borne by Owner
(subject to Contractor providing reasonable expense estimate).

STANDARD OF WORKS
14. (a) Computer-generated perspectives/plans/drawings are indicative only; tolerance of ±10%
of stipulated measurement values applies.
    (b) Natural materials (granite, marble, slate) vary in colour/tonality/grain/vein —
no absolute uniformity guaranteed.
15. Contractor removes tools/surplus materials and leaves Premises clean & tidy on completion
or termination, whichever earlier.
16. Contractor obtains Owner's written approval to drawings/material samples before commencing
Works; approved drawings/samples supersede prior communications.
17. Contractor provides a 12-month workmanship Warranty from the Completion Date; defects during
Warranty Period rectified at Contractor's own cost.
18. (a) Defects/shrinkage/faults notified in writing within Warranty Period (or up to 30 days
after expiration) shall be made good by Contractor at own expense within reasonable time.
    (b) Owner waives right to claim for defects not notified within 30 days of Warranty Period
expiration.
19. Warranty NOT valid where: (a) Works completed to Owner's satisfaction but full payment not
made; (b) Owner refuses to allow rectification works; (c) defects due to Owner's
misuse/wilful act/faulty workmanship; (d) damage from daily wear & tear/misuse; (e) modification
works carried out post-handover by Owner/others.
20. Contractor not liable for materials supplied by Owner or third parties not approved in writing.

TERMINATION
21. Contractor aware of circumstances causing >30-day delay must notify Owner in writing within
7 days, propose new timeframe within 7 calendar days; bears only directly-incurred additional
renovation costs due to delay (not indirect costs e.g. temporary accommodation).
22. If Works not of acceptable standard, or Contractor ceases work >30 consecutive days without
reasonable explanation, Owner may terminate by paying only for value of Works already performed.
23. If Agreement terminated by Owner through no fault of Contractor, Contractor may recover:
(a) value of Works already carried out (incl. materials/work prepared); and
(b) 5% of the Contract Sum (as damages, not penalty).

NOTICE
24. Notices sent by certificate of posting to address stated herein, or other agreed modes.

GENERAL
25. References to "Owner" mean each and every Owner; obligations are joint and several.
26. No failure/delay in exercising a right operates as a waiver.
27. Data kept confidential per Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act; written consent sought
for advertisement use.
28. Force majeure clause: Contractor excused from obligations during events beyond reasonable
control (neglect/default by Owner/third party, government action/inaction, delay in authority
approval, acts of God, strikes, war, riot, etc); Completion Date extended accordingly.
29. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of Singapore.

AS WITNESS the hands of the parties hereto the day and year first above written.

THE CONTRACTOR: ____________________ BUD STUDIO PTE. LTD.
THE OWNER: ____________________ NAME: Marcella Callista Risye
