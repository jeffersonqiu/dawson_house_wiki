# Living-Dining

Combined Living Room + Dining Room — open-plan per the moodboard's stated preference. The
furniture tracker, the Bud Studio scope, and the floor plan ("LIVING DINING" as one zone) all
treat it as a single space, so it's compiled here as one room.

## Design principles (from the J&M House Moodboard)

- Question whether a sofa is even needed (family spends more time at the dining table)
- Big dining table integrated into the living space, ideally behind/near the sofa
- All blinds: top-mounted, floor-length, end-to-end (bigger than window OK)
- Dedicated coffee corner
- TV wall kept clean/minimal — "just TV and TV console in similar colour to dining"
- Two-tone wall, floor-to-ceiling curtains, light grey carpet, dark greyish-brown extendable
  dining table with matching chairs
