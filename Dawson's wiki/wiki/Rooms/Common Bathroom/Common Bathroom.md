## 2nd design revision (Jaslyn meeting, 2026-06-13)

An Otter.ai transcript summary of a "2nd 3D design revision" review meeting
(`Dawson's wiki/inbox/Meeting with Jaslyn.md`) covered substantial NOT-YET-FINALISED design
discussion for this room:

- **Wall colour & texture**: a reddish-toned wall "to tie in with the lemon colour", using
  Subway tiles + wall lights to enhance the countertop; a lime wash / "lighthouse" textured
  finish for the walls, "similar to the wall tiles".
- **Vanity countertop + sink material**: a travertine countertop with a round undermount sink,
  "for easier cleaning/maintenance". This is NEW design scope — Appendix A has no countertop
  line item for this room (unlike [[Master Bathroom]], which has a contracted Lian Hin quartz
  countertop) and the Sink/Faucet rows are still $0.00 placeholders. See
  [[TASK-0008 - Confirm Common Bathroom vanity countertop material (travertine) and round undermount sink]].
- **Cabinet colour**: a darker bottom-half cabinet was discussed, plus a concern about contrast
  between white cabinets and a "red hair" material under daylight vs. artificial lighting. See
  [[TASK-0009 - Update Common Bathroom cabinet colour (darker bottom half, reduce contrast)]].
- **Sink size & storage**: an 80cm sink "for more functionality", plus a drying rack and drawer
  layout "for easy access". See
  [[TASK-0010 - Confirm Common Bathroom sink size (80cm) and drying-rack-drawer layout]].
- **Tile colour & grout**: a debate over tile colour saturation (recommending a more saturated
  hue) and a floor-tile mismatch concern ("the three pictures almost look like two different
  rooms"). The team also recommended **water-resistant grout for the shower/wet area**. See
  [[TASK-0011 - Upgrade Common Bathroom shower grout to water-resistant type]].
- **Mirror niche LED lighting**: add LED lighting in the niche/shelf area behind the mirror.
  Related (minor) discussion debated lighting colour temperature ("natural lighting looks
  cooler than artificial light"). See
  [[TASK-0012 - Add LED lighting in Common Bathroom mirror niche-shelf]].
- **Green wall / tile**: research the exact size and tile options for custom green wall tiles
  (whether they need to be custom-made), alongside a separate action item to update renders to
  a "lighter brown" wall colour instead of green (with matching flooring) — possibly the same
  surface under reconsideration, possibly two different elements. See
  [[TASK-0013 - Resolve Common Bathroom green wall — lighter-brown render update vs. tile research]].
- **Minor / not tracked as tasks**: shampoo-dish colour ("a fun, colorful option" suggested) —
  too minor to track individually.
