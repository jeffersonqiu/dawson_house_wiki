# Master Bedroom

"1 big master bedroom" was a stated top-level design principle (moodboard slide 2) — an
explicit tradeoff was even raised about whether to "open the glass door to make a bigger
living room, sacrificing master bedroom size" (slide 9, unresolved in the source material).

## Stated wants (moodboard slide 21)

- Doors should reveal both rooms on entry (no blocking wall/mirror)
- Dedicated vanity + skincare storage in the ensuite
- Must fit a king-size bed (matches the floor-plan annotation "KING SIZE BED 1900 X 2000")

## Design-review decisions / open items (first-draft render comments, pages 13–16)

- Parquet flooring colour to be adjusted to look more true-to-life ("more similar like IRL")
- **Wardrobe door finish: wood vs. white — UNDECIDED.** *"Open to keep it white if wood
  doesn't work."* This cascades into the TV-wall finish AND wall-paint colour decisions —
  see [[TASK-0004 - Resolve Master Bedroom wardrobe finish (wood vs white)]]
- Wall treatment: considering light-coloured limewash paint IF wardrobe doors go wood
- Wants a flushed horizontal "library" shelf element on the wall (referenced an inspiration image)
- **Full-length mirror placement — UNRESOLVED.** *"Is missing... open to suggestions on
  placement."* See [[TASK-0005 - Decide full-length mirror placement in Master Bedroom]]
- **Vanity/study desk: 1m width APPROVED** — *"actually 1m is ok!"* (see [[04 Decisions]])
