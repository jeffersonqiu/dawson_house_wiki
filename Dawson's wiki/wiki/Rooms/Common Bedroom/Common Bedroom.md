> INCONSISTENTLY across sources — "Study Room/Guest Bedroom" (moodboard v1, slide 26),
> "Bedroom 2" (moodboard v2, slides 21–22), "Guest Bedroom" (Reno Sheet furniture tracker),
> and **"Common Bedroom"** (signed Renovation Agreement, Appendix A, Section J item 10). The
> canonical name **"Common Bedroom"** was chosen to match the SIGNED CONTRACT's terminology —

## Stated design intent (moodboard slides 26–27, v2 slides 21–23, 25, 29)

- Convertible to guest bedroom on demand
- Upgradable to a kid's bedroom in 3–5 years (must comfortably fit a queen bed)
- Built-in study table, 1.4–1.6 m
- Full wardrobe wall
- Murphy bed (matches the room's dual guest/study/storage function)
- Colour scheme to mirror the Master Bedroom
- v2 adds an open idea: "sofa turned bed?" (slide 29 — not pursued/resolved further in source material)
