# TASK-0008 — Confirm Common Bathroom vanity countertop material (travertine) and round undermount sink

The 2nd design revision meeting discussed "a travertine countertop with a round undermount
sink for easier maintenance" for [[Common Bathroom]].

## Not a Change Order against the Kitchen

This was initially mis-attributed to the Kitchen (whose contracted countertop is Lian Hin
quartz, Appendix A Section K item 3, $2,035, itself marked "NEWLY EDITED"). On review, the
Kitchen's countertop/sink is unaffected — the Kitchen sink is the [[Sink]] (Zuhne Oslo
Workstation Sink, $788, already in the Furniture & Appliances tracker). See [[Kitchen]].

## New scope for Common Bathroom

Unlike [[Master Bathroom]] (which has a contracted Lian Hin quartz countertop, Appendix A
Section K item 4, $555), **Common Bathroom has no countertop/worktop line item in Appendix A**,
and its Sink/Faucet rows are still $0.00 placeholders in the tracker. A travertine
countertop + round undermount sink for this room would therefore be NEW scope requiring a
quote/Change Order conversation with Bud Studio (cost impact, clause 4) — not a swap of an
existing item.

## Needs confirming

- Travertine countertop spec (slab type, dimensions, finish)
- Round undermount sink model/size
- Resulting cost — new Section K + Sink/Faucet tracker entries
