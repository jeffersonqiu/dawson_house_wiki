## Learnings

- When a tracker swaps both the product AND price for an already-compiled item, the proposal
  should explicitly call out "SUPERSEDES previous record" in the `notes` field and ask the human
  to confirm it's a deliberate swap rather than a data-entry slip — especially when the price
  delta is large (here, desk went from $399 to $139, a >2.5x change).
- Renaming a note (e.g. "Table Lamps (Kave Malachi + Nuvira)" → "Table Lamp (Kave Malachi)") is
  itself a structural change that should go through the same approval gate as a price/status
  change — flagged explicitly in the proposal's "What to change" section.
